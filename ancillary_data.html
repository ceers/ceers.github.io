Simulated CEERS NIRCam imaging from Illustris TNG100

We provide simulated CEERS NIRCam images of the Extended Groth Strip (EGS) field in six filters. These images are constructed from IllustrisTNG100-1 and the Santa Cruz SAM, and are modified with noise from the JWST Exposure Time Calculator. Please see Rose et al. (2022) for details, and we ask that you cite this paper for any projects that make use of these images or products derived from them.

To access the images directly, visit this link. Please see the associated README describing the data. Note that the images linked below are each ~4 GB in size.

  • F115W fits image
  • F150W fits image
  • F200W fits image
  • F277W fits image
  • F356W fits image
  • F444W fits image
  • Catalog of source info and merger histories

NIRCam Imaging of TNG50 galaxies

We provide simulated NIRCam observations (F200W, F356W) of IllustrisTNG50 galaxies, particularly suited for morphological studies of high-redshift galaxies (3<z<6). Please see Costantin et al. (2022) for details, and we ask that you cite this paper for any projects that make use of these images or products derived from them.

  • Data are available at: www.lucacostantin.com/OMEGA.html#four
  • More info is available at: github.com/lcostant/OMEGA/tree/main/v1_CEERS
